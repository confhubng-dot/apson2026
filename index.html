🐘 About Yankari

Yankari Game Reserve, in southern Bauchi State, is Nigeria's premier wildlife destination — over 2,000 km² of savannah woodland first protected in the 1950s and long the country's best-known safari experience. It shelters one of West Africa's largest remaining elephant herds, alongside baboons, waterbuck, buffalo, hippos, crocodiles and hundreds of bird species.

♨️ Wikki Warm Springs

The jewel of Yankari — a crystal-clear natural spring that stays warm (~31°C) all year round. Delegates can swim in the spring after the game drive; it is a short walk from the main camp. Bring swimwear!

🚙 What to Expect on the Excursion

The conference visit holds on Saturday 26th September. Expect a guided safari game drive in open-top trucks, a visit to Wikki Warm Springs, and time for photos and refreshments at the Wikki Camp. Yankari is roughly a 1½–2 hour drive from Bauchi town — the LOC will announce departure times and transport arrangements.

🎒 What to Bring

Comfortable clothing and shoes, hat and sunscreen, swimwear and a towel for the springs, camera or charged phone, and some cash for souvenirs and refreshments.
